Missouri

Missouri is a state in the Midwestern region of the United States, known for its diverse geography and rich history. It is bordered by eight states and has a total area of 69,715 square miles. The state features a mix of glaciated plains, the Ozark Mountains, and the Missouri River, which flows through its center. Missouri has a population of over six million residents and is divided into 114 counties and the independent city of St. Louis.
